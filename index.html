Episode I

THE CODER'S MENACE


In a parallel universe known as Academic Semester, our protagonist embarks on a mission not unlike training a Jedi. As a student tasked with a formidable challenge for the hallowed halls of college, the mission is clear: create a web page using the ancient HTML language.

The teacher, as wise as Master Yoda, imparts knowledge similar to the teachings of the Force, guiding the student through the complexities of HTML tags and cascading style sheets. The task becomes a lightsaber in our young padawan's hands, each line of code a step closer to mastering the digital force.
